About ROBLOX-Ayro

Ayro is a FREE to use System that makes Aiming better for Consoles & Phone players

How to install

To start and install you'll need a few Objects

LocalScript (Call it whatever you want, or just Ayro) | AyroTeamKilling (BoolValue, Default: FALSE) - Checking for teams and if they are not on your team | AyroTargetMode (StringValue, Default: DISTANCEBASED) - Used for choosing the Aiming targets | AyroShowHealth (BoolValue, Default: FALSE) - Shows Ayro's Target about it's health | AyroRange (NumberValue, Default: 60 (= 6.0 studs)) | AyroPredictMovement (BoolValue, DEFAULT: FALSE) - NOT USED YET (Only available in a testing version that i have access)

Or you can use a Package Link: 128496506090743
