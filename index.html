How does Ayro Work and Enable itself?

Ayro is dependent on whether you have:

  • No keyboard plugged in, OR
  • A controller connected

It will turn your camera smoothly to your opponent.

How does it choose your target?

As explained in the "How to Install" segment, you have the RANGE and Target mode options. It chooses via your target mode and its configs, then will loop between all targets in certain folders (thanks to ListTarget table inside the code).
